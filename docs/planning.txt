SoulSell

A simple website with these pages
- landing page (where categories , products and collections are shown)
- category detail page (banners on top , products beneath )
- shop page (all products listed)
- collection page (banner on top, product beneath)
- product detail page (all details of a single product)
- cart page (with products in cart details)
- checkout page (with billing address, payments , product info , user information)
- thank you (with nice animations)
- user accounts dashboard
- product tracking page

Expected Models
- category
- product
- product images
- collection
- collection_products
- users
- order
- order_products
